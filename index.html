The Pipeline Conference

As Programming Chair in both 2021 and 2022, I oversaw the team responsible for orchestrating the events that lay at the heart of the conference.

I worked with the board to figure out which event ideas were most popular, then directed the team in the tasks of fleshing them out, finding speakers, and expertly managing each step of an event's production down to tech-checks etc.

The goal was to ensure speakers knew exactly what to expect and were prepared for their talks on the day-of, which, not only would the audience enjoy listening to, but the speakers enjoyed giving.

Since events were virtual I introduced the use of Slack as a 'green room' where speakers could check-in before their events and coordinate amongst themselves, and their event moderator. .

LA ACM SIGGRAPH

As Chair during Lockdown (2021 - 2022), my foremost objective was to sustain momentum by not missing a single month. Something many groups struggled with, often to find it arduous to reemerge, if at all, after Lockdown.

I was responsible for organizing cutting-edge, timely, events such as AI in VFX, and Robot Computer Graphics, drawing such large audiences, we decided to raise the chapter's Zoom limit. And when I did, The VFX of Dune, our LinkedIn sign ups grew by over 10x.

Dealing with companies such as Pixar, Warner, NVIDIA, and DNEG, was commonplace and I created informative, graphically stimulating, decks that quickly gave them the information they needed about the organization.

Animae Caribe

Facing an unexpected shift online during Lockdown, the animation festival hired me to create a fun day for it's closing. I crafted interesting experiences to compensate for the absence of in-person engagement using platorms such as Altspace for the closing event, attracting the most viewers on Altspace during it's time period.

A High Fidelity island map as a lounge attendees could roam between events, and Discord to host such events as a game tournament further enriched the experience,

I art directed a talented team to create graphics tailored to each platform. And our innovative approach was noted in other islands of the Caribbean by events also trying to figure out ways to move their events online.
